Game Description

Your ability to remember will be what decides your fate. Hidden within these cards, is the ultimate killing spell, which will automatically end your opponent. Matching two spell cards together will cast that spell and reduce or add the corresponding points from your or your opponent's health. Different spells have different effects. Each witch or wizard begins with 20 points. The game ends when someone runs out of health or when Avada Kedavra is cast.

  • Spells and their results:
    • Avada Kedavra - Automatic kill and ends the game.
    • Crucio - minus 7 points from opponent
    • Expulso - minus 5 points from opponent
    • Incendio - minus 4 points from opponent
    • Liberacorpus - gives back points from previous spell
    • Stupefy - minus 2 points from opponent
    • Rennervate - adds 3 points to your health
    • Expelliarmus - minus 2 points from opponent
    • Finite Incantatum - adds 2 points to your health
